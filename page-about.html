History of our company

2022

The Beginning of ASCO's journey in USA

ASCO entered the U.S. market and immediately stood out for the superior quality of its products. After thoroughly analyzing the market, we selected the best window and door manufacturers in Europe, providing our customers with reliable products. From the very first year, we operated under American contracts, eliminating any risks for our clients.

2023

ASCO Enhances Market Presence with Full-Service Solutions

Our company has strengthened its market position by increasing window and door deliveries and assembling a skilled team of measurers and installers. This year’s highlight has been our full-service approach—covering measurement, delivery, installation, and post-warranty support—boosting customer trust and reinforcing our reputation as a reliable U.S. partner.

2024

Expanding U.S. Presence with Aluplast Partnership

We continue to enhance our presence in the U.S. market with high-quality European windows and doors and improved customer service. This year, we signed a contract with Aluplast to supply their windows in the U.S., expanding our product range. With additional partners, we've made our prices more competitive while maintaining top quality. Our team now includes specialists for post-warranty services, and each client receives a personal manager available 24/7.
