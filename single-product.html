Technologies

Technologies used in our IDEAL® 4000

Bonding technology

Originally used in vehicle production, glass gluing technology has been innovatively adapted by aluplast for window manufacturing. This advanced method involves bonding the glass unit to the window profile on all sides with a strong, durable adhesive.

Key Benefits:

  • Superior Security: Provides a secure and reliable connection between the glass and sash, meeting the RC2 standard for burglary protection.
  • Enhanced Durability: Prevents the window frame from being squeezed, ensuring long-lasting form-resistance.
  • Effortless Operation: Keeps the window opening and closing smoothly over time.

Powerdur inside

The special material reinforced with fiberglass, powerdur, ensures the stability of the profile and eliminates the need for steel reinforcement. A profile with powerdur® technology instead of steel achieves significantly better sound insulation.

Key Benefits:

  • Lightweight: A window using powerdur® and bonding inside technologies is 20% lighter than standard windows, despite its enhanced stability.
  • Eco-friendliness: The reduced weight makes transportation easier and helps reduce environmental impact.
  • Improved sound insulation
  • Easier transportation

Foam inside

The innovative foam inside technology enables sensational thermal insulation! After welding, the central chambers of the frame and sash are filled with polyurethane foam, a material well-proven in the insulation industry, which aluplast has successfully implemented in window technologies.

Key Benefits:

  • Exceptional Insulation: Thanks to the excellent insulating properties of polyurethane, it is used in refrigeration and freezer units, as well as in flooring and other sealed materials.
  • Eco-friendly Recycling: During recycling, the polyurethane foam can be completely separated from other materials and reused in production.
  • Outstanding thermal insulation
  • Versatile use in insulation materials

Safetec Inside

Effectively prevent break-in attempts. Thanks to the solid central web of the third sealing contour, the frame not only improves energy efficiency but also prevents the quick forcing of the window sash.

Key Advantages:

  • Enhanced Security: The solid central web ensures stronger protection against break-ins.
  • Energy Efficiency: Alongside security, it also contributes to better energy-saving performance.
  • Breathe easy knowing your windows are secure
